Office Hours

Your instructor or TA will be available Monday-Friday from 9pm to 5pm at minimum, with the exception of one hour between 1-2pm, which is reserved by the staff for planning. During lab time, you can schedule one-on-one time with either of us. After hours, we may be available in the classroom or in HipChat, which we use for group communication.
