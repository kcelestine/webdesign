January 2015

Here you will find a list of topics to be covered and a rough outline of when we will hit each item. Class objectives will begin with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and then move into web design principles and practice.

You'll notice that the number of new topics slowly decreases as the weeks progress. That's because the amount of work we're producing should steadily increase with time. I've also left some wiggle room near week 9 to review previous topics or explore new items in greater detail. The key to success here: Iterate, iterate, iterate. Always be creating.™
