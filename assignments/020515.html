Assignments for Thursday, February 5

Target.com Category Page UX

Example current category page on target.com

Target Corporation is seeking help redesigning the landing page experience for their online retail store, target.com. Their analytics have revealed some performance concerns and they want a design that restructures the page content to emphasize Target exclusive brands and most searched items. They would also like a design that differentiates between featured products, subcategory listings, popular picks, and exclusive brands.

View project brief

  • Begin sketching and designing ideas to improve the user experience of the category page.
  • Pull images and content from target.com as needed.
  • Keep the header the same as the live site, but consider responsive views.
    You do NOT need to recreate their elaborate "shop all categories" navigation.
  • Additional logo files available here.
  • Build the page using HTML and Sass. Make it responsive.

Final HTML build for the Target.com Category Page is due Monday, February 9 at 8:00am.
